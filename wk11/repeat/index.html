Computational Aesthetics

Repetition is the theme of this project. I chose repetition because the authors of Form + Code describe repetition as fundamental to the origins and purposes of programming. To make the project I derived the nesting doll function from the owl design in our text. I created the repetition with a for loop.

With more time, I would change the angles and locations of the dolls rather than having them all together in one line.
